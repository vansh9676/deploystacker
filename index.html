FAQs

Get answers to common questions

How long does it take to build a website?

+
A standard business website typically takes 2–4 weeks. Complex web applications or SaaS platforms can take 6–16 weeks depending on scope and features.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

+
Yes, all our packages include post-launch support. We also offer dedicated monthly maintenance plans for updates, security patches, and performance monitoring.

What technologies do you use for development?

+
We work with React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AWS, and more. We choose the best stack based on your project's specific requirements.

Can you help with AI automation for my business?

+
Absolutely. We build custom AI automation solutions using OpenAI, LangChain, and other AI frameworks to automate repetitive tasks, customer support, data processing, and more.

Do you work with international clients?

+
Yes, we work with clients worldwide. We communicate via email, Slack, and video calls, and we adapt to your timezone for meetings and updates.

What is your payment structure?

+
We typically work with a 50% upfront deposit and 50% on project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payment schedules.
