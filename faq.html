Project & Process

How long does it take to build a website?

+
A standard business website typically takes 2–4 weeks. Complex web applications or SaaS platforms can take 6–16 weeks depending on scope and features required.

What is your development process?

+
We follow a 4-step process: Discovery & Strategy → Design & Prototype → Development & Build → Launch & Support. You're involved and informed at every stage.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

+
Yes. We integrate user-friendly CMS solutions so you can update content, add blog posts, and manage your site without any technical knowledge.

What technologies do you use?

+
We work with React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AWS, Docker, and more. We choose the best stack based on your project's specific requirements and goals.

Support & Maintenance
