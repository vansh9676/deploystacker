Pricing FAQs

Common pricing questions

Are there any hidden fees?

+
No hidden fees ever. The price you see is the price you pay. Any additional scope changes are discussed and agreed upon before work begins.

What is the payment structure?

+
We work with a 50% upfront deposit and 50% on project completion. For larger enterprise projects, we can arrange milestone-based payment schedules.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

+
Absolutely. You can start with a Starter plan and upgrade to Professional or Enterprise as your business grows. We'll handle the transition seamlessly.

Do you offer custom quotes?

+
Yes. If your project doesn't fit neatly into one of our plans, we're happy to provide a custom quote tailored to your specific requirements.
